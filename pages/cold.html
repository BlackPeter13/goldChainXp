Certificate Of Ledger Deposit

Fango's DeFi Banking Suite & Asset Laboratory

The purpose of COLD DeFi Banking Suite is to create an in-house interest market for XFG deposit yield. Allowing for multiple off-chain assets to serve as interest on time-locked XFG ledger deposits - while simultaneously preserving Fango's fixed supply & sound money principals.

Below is a list of details on all off-chain asset(s) offered as interest options thru COLD Banking Suite.
