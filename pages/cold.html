Certificate Of Ledger Deposits

Fango's DeFi Banking Suite & Asset Laboratory

The purpose of COLD Banking Suite is to create an in-house DeFi money market for XFG deposit yield.

Allowing XFG to function as a yield-bearing asset, which will not only remove some dependancy upon centralized exchanges ( holders can borrow against or sell tokens representing XFG deposit yield in a DeFi environment; without ever trading their XFG ) but more importantly, continues preserving the original hard supply and sound money principals of Fango.

Details on all off-chain tokens to be offered thru COLD will be listed below.
